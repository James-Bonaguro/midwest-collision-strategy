The Economic Vise

This is the core of the solvency crisis. The cost of doing business (parts, energy, software) has risen faster than insurer reimbursement rates, compressing margins from both sides.

Labor: From Shortage to Burden

The "technician shortage" panic drove wages to unsustainable highs. Now, as volume normalizes, many shops are overstaffed with expensive labor that isn't turning enough hours.

Vehicle complexity and high salvage values are pushing more cars to "Total Loss." This removes high-dollar repairs from the shop floor, often after administrative time has already been spent.

The Exit Strategy Bottleneck

An Aging Ownership

Nearly 40% of independent owners are over 60. The succession crisis is looming just as the buyer pool shrinks.

The "Big Buy" is Over

High interest rates have cooled private equity and MSO (Multi-Shop Operator) acquisitions. The "lottery ticket" exit strategy of 2021 is no longer reliable.
